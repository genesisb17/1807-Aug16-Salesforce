Welcome to the Pokemon World Tournament!

The rules are simple. Once you register in the tournament, you will receive a team of 6 random pokemon. Upon receiving your team you may review the information about each pokemon, and you may mulligan one of the pokemon on your team for another random pokemon. After each battle, the winning trainer may choose to exchange one of their pokemon for one of the loser's pokemon. In addition Judges award points during battles based on style, style points are awarded for the following actions:

  • Same type attack bonus
  • Super effective attacks
  • Creating weather effects
  • One-hit KOs
  • Impressing the Judges introduces a style point multiplier
Every 5 rounds, Players may spend points earned from the Judges on:
  • Held Items
  • Training their Pokemon new moves
  • Replacing their Pokemon
